310V Battery Management System

A commercial-grade, high-voltage Battery Management System migrated to a Lockstep ARM Cortex-M7 microcontroller. This board handles cell balancing, charging, and protection for a 310V automotive battery pack, achieving ISO 26262 functional safety compliance for deployment in production vehicles.

Key Highlights

Lockstep ARM Cortex-M7

Migrated the full BMS firmware to a lockstep dual-core ARM Cortex-M7, enabling hardware-level fault detection for safety-critical operation.

ISO 26262 Compliance

Achieved automotive functional safety certification, meeting rigorous hardware and software integrity requirements for high-voltage systems.

GDB Debugging Pipeline

Utilized GDB-based debugging workflows for low-level firmware bring-up and validation of critical safety paths on the Cortex-M7.

310V High-Voltage Design

Managed cell balancing, overcurrent protection, and thermal monitoring for a 310V automotive battery pack across the full operating envelope.
