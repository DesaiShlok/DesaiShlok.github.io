Battery Health Monitor

A custom PCB designed to monitor the health of an 8S lithium-ion drone battery pack. The board tracks State of Charge (SoC) across all cells and connects to the internet via MQTT to stream real-time telemetry data for remote battery pack monitoring and diagnostics.

Key Highlights

8S Battery Pack Monitoring

Monitors individual cell voltages and pack-level State of Charge for an 8-series lithium-ion battery configuration.

MQTT Telemetry

Publishes real-time SoC, voltage, and health data over MQTT, enabling cloud-based dashboards and remote monitoring.

Custom Altium PCB

Designed the full schematic and PCB layout in Altium Designer, optimized for the high-voltage sensing and compact form factor.

Internet-Connected

Onboard connectivity enables over-the-air data transmission, removing the need for local wired readouts of battery status.
