Automotive IoT Telemetry Module

A commercial automotive IoT tracking module taken from schematic design through to at-scale fleet deployment. The board combines cellular connectivity, accelerometer-based motion detection, and aggressive power management to deliver reliable vehicle telemetry across thousands of active units.

Key Highlights

Fleet-Scale Cellular Optimization

Scaled to 2,000+ active devices by optimizing cellular packet sizes, reducing bandwidth overhead while maintaining reliable data uplinks.

Hardware-Interrupt Wake Triggers

Deployed hardware-interrupt based wake triggers that cut power consumption by 74%, enabling long-duration battery-powered fleet tracking.

Vibration Algorithm

Engineered a time-series vibration algorithm to filter raw accelerometer noise, detecting vehicle movement with 98% accuracy.

Full Product Lifecycle

Took the hardware from initial schematic design and PCB layout through prototype validation and into at-scale production deployment.
